Latest From us

Over 80% Indian engineers are unemployable, lack new-age technology skills: Report

A report by Aspiring Minds found that over 80 percent of engineering graduates in India are unemployable due to a lack of necessary technological skills. The research, which included students from India, the US, and China, highlighted that few students engage in projects beyond their curriculum or internships. Varun Aggarwal, CTO and co-founder of Aspiring Minds, emphasized the need for better industry exposure and alignment of incentives for all stakeholders to address this issue.
